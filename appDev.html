MoodTracker

MoodTracker is a social media app aimed towards the introvers. We wanted to create a self-sufficient experience for the user without confusing them. We made it easy to connect to people, but took steps to ensure privacy. We made it fun and but not addictive. With all the bells and whistles but none of the costs!
